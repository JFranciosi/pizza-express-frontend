5. Fair Play & Game Algorithm

Pizza Express utilizes a Provably Fair system to guarantee transparency and fairness. The outcome of each game round is pre-determined using a cryptographic hashing algorithm (SHA-256) and cannot be altered once the round has started.

How it works:
The crash point is generated by combining a Server Seed (kept secret initially) and a Client Seed (typically a hash of a block or public input). This generates a result that is completely random and verifiable.

Winning Probabilities:
The theoretical Return to Player (RTP) is approximately 99%. The remaining 1% represents the "House Edge," ensuring the sustainability of the platform.
